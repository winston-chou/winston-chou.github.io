Winston Chou

Hi there,

I'm a Staff Data Scientist at Apple, where I work on experimentation systems and methodology.

Prior to joining Apple, I was a Research Scientist at Facebook, where I worked on missing data problems in the context of advertising measurement.

I received my PhD in political science from Princeton University, where I was fortunate to work with Rafaela Dancygier and Kosuke Imai. I studied voting decisions and survey methodology and eventually wrote a dissertation on electoral politics in Europe.

My wife, Katherine McCabe, teaches American politics and data science at Rutgers University.
